Classic Homemade Lasagna

back to Home

Description

This classic homemade lasagna is the ultimate comfort food. Layered with a rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and plenty of melted cheese, this dish is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

The recipe combines traditional Italian flavors with an easy-to-follow process, making it suitable for both beginners and experienced home cooks.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic and sauté briefly, then add ground beef. Cook until browned.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, then add layers of noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and repeat.
  7. Top with remaining sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 10 minutes until cheese is golden.
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
